About Us

The barbershop was established in 2014. It all started as a hobby and I used to cut my friends on a regular basis and as time went by a lot of people started to notice those hairstyles my friends had and were asked about them.

So a thought came into my mind that I should open a barbershop and that was in 2014, now we are here.

We are currently located at 456 6th Ave, Rustenburg, North West 0299.
